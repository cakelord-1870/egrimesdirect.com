A reputation built over time.

While Grimes Industrial remains one of the premier distributors of 3M Canadas renowned products, over the years we have diversified & expanded our lines.

We now carry a comprehensive variety of industrial and consumer products. Reflecting that continued growth and our commitment to innovation, in 1994 we launched eGrimesDirect.com, an online representation of our business.

Further expansion then led to a company name change to better reflect our Brand and in 1998 the company became Grimes Industrial Products Group.

Grimes Industrial is your source for wholesale safety and industrial products. Grimes industrial is one of the top ranked supply companies, known for providing expert advice as a trusted supplier of wholesale safety, inudstrial supplies and  equipment.
