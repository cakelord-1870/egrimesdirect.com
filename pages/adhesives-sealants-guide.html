OVERVIEW OF THE BENEFITS OF ADHESIVES & SEALANTS:

New manufacturing components have challenged the role & application of adhesives & sealants.

Think about joining different new materials to construct a new or improved component.

Which joining technique will be best?

Can new joining techniques solve well-known joining problems such as:

Welding can alter the properties of the material within the hat affected area.
Riveting can allow force transfer points.
Drilling holes can damage & weaken the material.

The answer is YES, in most cases!

Bonding technology currently plays a critical role in the manufacturing sector efficiencies & growth.

The Top Four Reasons to consider updating your bonding process: 

Material - Bonding technology can bond any desired combination of materials with each other to create long lasting bonds.

Processing - Bonding technology in production processes allows the material properties of the substrates to remain intact.

Joining - New construction methods can be explored as the long lasting bonds & stable strength of the substrates can handle more stress.

Design - Bonding technology can introduce customized enhancements to the component during the actual joining process.

Times Savings = Cost Savings!

Adhesive & Sealant Types & Common Uses

Acrylic Based Adhesives

Acrylic Adhesives are used for bonding most plastics, steel, aluminum and oily metal surfaces.
View details

Adhesion Promoters

Adhesion promoters are used to create a strong bond on surfaces that are traditionally difficult to adhere to such as paint and metals.
View details

Adhesive Activators

Adhesive activators are used to speed up the dry time of adhesives.
View details

Adhesive Primers

Adhesive primers are used to increase the adhesion of metal and glass when a stronger bond is required.
View details

Adhesive Sealers & Sealants

Adhesive sealers and sealants provides a strong barrier to protect surfaces from substances and liquids.

Some popular adhesive sealants include:

Scotchweld 4693, a high performance adhesive that forms strong bonds on most plastics, polypropylene, polyethylene and polyolefin blends. It bonds well to other plastics such as ABS, PMMA & nylons, polyesters, rigid polystyrene & resists weathering, water & cleaning products.

Adhesive Sealants 550 / 730 / 760.3M 550 Polyurethane comes in grey, white or black, 730 Low VOC comes in clear and 760 Low VOC comes in white. These adhesive sealante are formulated to have a wide variety of hardness, open times & performance parameters to meet many applications requirements.

5200 Marine grade Adhesive Sealant is a fast-curing marine polyurethane adhesive sealant which becomes tack-free in one hour & fully cures in approx. 24 hours.

View details

Aerosol Adhesives & Sealants

Aerosol spray on adhesives and sealants are mainly used on plastics, metals, fabrics, and glass.
View details

Artist & Craft Adhesives

Artist and craft adhesives and sealants are great for hobby and artistic project. Each project carries its own unique properties.
View details

Construction Structural Sealants

Construction Structural sealants are used to create a strong bond on plastics, metals and glass.
View details

Contact Adhesives

Contact adhesives can be sprayed on, rolled on and brushed on and are used to provide a very strong initial bond.
View details

Epoxies & Two Part Adhesives

Epoxies are a fast setting and low viscosity adhesive. Two part adhesives are typically flexible as well as being high strength while providing a longer work life.
View details

Hot Melt Adhesives

Hot melt adhesives are one part adhesives used to bond wood and plastics to other plastics as well as metals and glass.
View details

Instant Adhesives (Cyanoacrylate)

Instant adhesives or cyanoacrylate, also known as crazy glue is best used when a strong instant bond is required.
View details

Retaining Compounds & Threadlockers

Threadlocker adhesives provide a permanent high strength bond for locking bolts and studs. Retaining compounds prevent spin out if rigid assemblies such as locking sleeves and bushings.
View details

Rubber & Gasket Adhesives

Powerful adhesives used for rubber and gaskets.
View details

Silicones

Silicone cures to a tough rubbery solid and can bond with wood, ceramic, fibers and many plastics.
View details

Spray Adhesives & Spray Glues

A wide variety of adhesives and sealants are available in a sprayable format.
View details

Urethane Adhesives & Sealants

Urethane adhesives are high performance and highly resistant to impact.
View details
